Who we are

A foundation built on one belief: change happens together.

Every registration below exists for one reason — so donors and partners can trust exactly where their support goes.

Section 8 Company 80G Certified 12A Certified CSR-1 Registered NITI Aayog Registered ISO 9001:2015
Founded on 23 September 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO), the foundation is headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh, and now runs programmes with volunteers across the country.

InAmigos Foundation works with a network of dedicated volunteers and corporate partners to run six focused initiatives spanning food security, education, animal welfare, women's empowerment, environmental sustainability and livelihood skilling.

Our approach stays close to the ground: local volunteers identify the need, our teams design the response, and our corporate and individual supporters make it possible. Every contribution — a meal packed, a class taught, an animal rescued, a sapling planted — adds up to a more inclusive and compassionate society.

Together, we're building a foundation that villages and neighbourhoods across India can genuinely rely on.

Beneficiaries

Six projects, one shared root.

Each initiative tackles a different need, but every one is powered by the same volunteers and the same belief in collective action.

Project Seva

Food & clothing for the underprivileged

Regular food and clothing drives that reach families facing hardship, delivered directly by our volunteer network on the ground.

Project Bachpanshala

Quality education for children

Nurturing young minds through learning support and school resources, so every child gets a genuine shot at a bright future.

Project Jeev

Animal welfare & rescue

Rescue, protection and feeding programmes for animals in need — because compassion doesn't stop with people.

Project Udaan

Women's empowerment

Skill development and financial-independence training that helps women build livelihoods on their own terms.

Project Prakriti

Environmental conservation

Tree plantation drives and sustainability efforts that work toward a cleaner, greener tomorrow for every community we serve.

Project Vikas

Skill & livelihood development

Employability-focused skill-building programmes that open doors to stable, dignified work.
